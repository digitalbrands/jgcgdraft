About Jayaditya CGC Origin

Jayaditya CGC established in the year 2019 (In collaboration with CGC - Madhapur) set up by a parent and a group of professionals engaged in helping those diagnosed with autism & ADHD to lead full and enriched lives and become valuable members of the community they live in. Dedicated to enriching the lives of people with special needs, Jayaditya CGC seeks to share knowledge and expertise with parents/caretakers and other professionals to supports the development and dissemination of skills/strategies needed to provide the best care and support for people with autism At Jayaditya CGC, our experts treat developmental disabilities, syndromes caused by genetic disorders, cerebral palsy, slow learners, speech and communication issues etc. We focus on detailed assessment, provide full-fledged advanced treatment approaches by closely monitoring children needs. Our treatments are customized to best fit the need of every child. We have been serving around 30+ children everyday with ADHD/ASD, neuro and motor disabilities and overall 100+ children since inception.

About Our Founder

Krishna Mohan is a result-oriented professional with 20+ years of diverse work experience. An IIM Calcutta Alumni, he started his career at ADP Pvt Ltd, a leading organization in the Payroll industry, and rose to senior leadership positions. With expertise in Banking, Payroll, and Psychology sectors, he embodies Servant Leadership in his management style. With 14+ years of leadership experience, he serves as a pillar in the leadership team, offering support and guidance to associates across the organization, regardless of their team affiliation. Krishna Mohan is a role model and mentor, motivating others in their career development. He also excels as a motivational speaker, conducting sessions for associates, schools, and external events.

Krishna Mohan, drawing from his personal life experiences, made a life-changing decision to leave the corporate world and embark on a new journey. He founded M/S Jayaditya Child Guidance Centre, an organization dedicated to creating awareness and providing services to children with Special Needs. The centre offers therapy services for special needs children and valuable parental guidance. Krishna's primary objective is to raise awareness in society about Autism & ADHD and establish a supportive environment for these children. He understands the challenges parents face due to social stigma and aims to offer them counselling and support. By focusing on both the children and their parents, Krishna is making a meaningful impact in the lives of those dealing with Special Needs.
